Cybitrage

A currency arbitrage detector using real-time stock market data from 1forge.com. Applying the Bellman-Ford algorithm to detect negative weight cycles on graphs, arbitrage opportunites are identified and displayed.

Insert which currencies to use by typing their acronyms in a comma-separated list. Cybitrage will identify if any arbitrage opportunities exist between them.
